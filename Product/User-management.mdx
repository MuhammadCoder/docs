---

## For existing users (inviting someone):

1. Log in: [**Visito Login**](https://app.visitoai.com/auth/login)
2. Click your company name (top right corner)
3. Go to [**Settings**](https://app.visitoai.com/settings)
4. Enter the new user's email under **Invite member**
5. Choose access level:
   - **Admin** = Full access
   - **User** = Can view & reply to messages
6. Click **Invite**

---

## For invited users:

1. Check your inbox for a Visito email (from **no-reply**)
2. Click **Accept Invitation**
3. Set a password and log in

<Note>
  If you’re invited to multiple Visito accounts, accept each invitation to access all assigned teams.
</Note>

---

## Reset Your Password

### Steps to Reset

1. Log in: [**Visito Login**](https://app.visitoai.com/auth/login)
2. Click on **“Forgot Password – Click Here”**
3. Enter the email you used to create your Visito account
4. Check your inbox for an email from [**no-reply@visito.ai**](mailto:no-reply@visito.ai)
5. Open the email and click the **"Recover your password"** button
6. On the new page, enter and confirm your new password — and you're done\!
