## Steps to Connect

1. **Open this link to start:** [**bit.ly/visito-app-cloudbeds**](https://bit.ly/visito-app-cloudbeds)
2. **Log in to your Cloudbeds account**
3. **Grant permissions** to Visito when prompted

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot_2024-08-19_at_1.34.28_p.m..png"
  alt="Screenshot"
  style={{ height:"200px" }}
/>

1. **Confirm the connection** here: [**Integrations Page**](https://app.visitoai.com/settings/integrations)

---

## Troubleshooting (If the connection fails)

1. **Log in to Cloudbeds: **[**hotels.cloudbeds.com/auth/login**](https://hotels.cloudbeds.com/auth/login)
2. **Go to:**

   `Configuration > Marketplace > Apps`

   Search for **Visito**, then click **More Information**
