## Requirements

- Admin access to your website (e.g., Webflow, Wix, WordPress)
- Ability to add code to the `<head>` section

---

## Step-by-Step Setup

1. **Log in to Visito:**

   <Info>
     [**https://app.visitoai.com/auth/login**](https://app.visitoai.com/auth/login**)
   </Info>
2. \*\*Go to \*\*[**Connect**](https://app.visitoai.com/connect) and select:

   <Info>
     → Embed \> Copy to get your Webchat code
   </Info>
3. **Open your website settings** and locate the **Head section**

   <Info>
     Usually found under **Settings**, **Custom Code**, or **SEO**
   </Info>
4. **Paste the code** into the `<head>` of your site
5. **Save and publish** your changes

---
